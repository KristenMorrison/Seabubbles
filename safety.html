Safety Information

Small Groups

We limit the number of customers we take on each dive to a maximum of four. This allows us to provide personalized attention to each customer and ensures a safe and enjoyable experience.

Licensed Captain

Our boat captain is licensed and experienced in navigating the waters around Waikiki. You can trust that you're in good hands with our captain.

Equipment Maintenance

We understand the importance of having well-maintained equipment. We regularly check and maintain all of our diving equipment to ensure it's in top condition.

PADI Registered

We're registered with PADI, the Professional Association of Diving Instructors. This means that we adhere to strict safety standards and guidelines.

Customer Health and Waivers

The safety of our customers is our top priority. We require all customers to be in good health and sign a waiver before diving.

Master Certification and Experience

Our lead diving instructor has a master certification and 15 years of experience. You can trust that you're in expert hands with us.
