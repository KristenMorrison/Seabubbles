Aloha Welcome

Aloha and welcome to our dive company! We are thrilled to have you here and excited to introduce you to the wonders of the underwater world. Our team of experienced and passionate instructors are here to guide you through every step of your diving journey. Whether you're a seasoned diver or a complete beginner, we have a range of courses and expeditions that cater to all levels. Explore the colorful reefs and diverse marine life of our beautiful location with us. Safety is our top priority, and we pride ourselves on providing a fun, friendly and professional diving experience. Let's dive in together!

Small Personal Group Tours!

Experience the underwater world in an intimate and personalized setting. With a maximum group size of four people, our experienced instructors provide a safe and enjoyable diving experience. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced diver, book your tour today and discover the magic of the underwater world.

Experience the Wildlife of Waikiki!

Discover the natural beauty and diverse wildlife that thrives in this bustling city. From colorful tropical fish to majestic sea turtles, our experienced guides will take you on a journey through the crystal-clear waters that surround Waikiki. Our tours aim to inspire a love and appreciation for the natural world.

Just a Walk Away!!

Being where we moorn our boat, you won't have to travel far to experience the magic of the underwater world. And for those who prefer a ride, our shuttle transportation service will take you to and from your hotel, ensuring a stress-free and enjoyable experience. So why wait? Book your dive adventure today and discover the wonders that lie beneath the crystal-clear waters of Waikiki. We're just a walk away!
